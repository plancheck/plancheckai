How It Works

Three steps to a
complete plan check

No training required. No complex setup. Upload your plan and get a professional compliance report in seconds.

STEP 01
📤
Upload Your Plan

Upload any construction plan document — PDF, scanned image, or digital drawing. Architectural sheets, fire protection plans, site plans, and more.

STEP 02
🧠
AI Analyzes the Drawing

Our AI reads every dimension, annotation, room label, and symbol on the plan. It interprets the drawing the way a trained plan checker would — then cross-references it against applicable codes.

STEP 03
📋
Receive Your Report

Get a full compliance report with every finding categorized by severity, exact code citations, specific locations on the plan, corrective recommendations, and a list of items requiring manual verification.

Capabilities

Everything a plan check
should include

🔍
Reads Actual Drawings

AI reads dimensions, annotations, room labels, door schedules, and symbols directly from your plan documents — not just metadata.

⚖️
Exact Code Citations

Every finding references the specific IBC, IFC, or ADA section that applies. No vague flags — every issue is traceable to a code requirement.

🎯
Severity Classification

Findings are categorized as Critical, Major, Minor, or Pass — so plan checkers and applicants know exactly what to prioritize.

Transparency on Limits

The system tells you exactly what it couldn't review and why — with risk levels and instructions for manual verification. No false confidence.

📊
Coverage Meter

Every report includes an AI coverage percentage — showing how much of the submission was reviewable and what requires human follow-up.

Exportable Reports

Generate and print professional compliance reports to share with applicants, teams, or project stakeholders.
